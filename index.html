Culture & Traditions

Algerian culture is a mix of Arab, Amazigh and Mediterranean influences. Traditional music, clothing and festivals reflect the country's diverse heritage.

Food & Cuisine

Algerian cuisine includes couscous, tajine and fresh Mediterranean dishes. Meals are often shared with family and friends.
